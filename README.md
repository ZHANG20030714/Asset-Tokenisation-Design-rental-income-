# Asset Tokenisation Design: Fractionalised Rental Income

**Module:** Decentralised Finance and Blockchain (IFTE0007)  
**Institution:** University College London (UCL)  

## 1. Project Overview

This repository contains the smart contract prototype for an asset tokenisation scheme. The project models the tokenisation of future rental cash flows generated by a pool of residential properties. 

By representing these cash flows as Fungible Tokens (FTs) on a public blockchain, the design aims to solve traditional real estate market frictions, such as high barriers to entry, illiquidity, and opaque cash flow distribution. The logic follows a clear chain: **Asset (Rental Property) → Token (RNT) → Market (DeFi Distribution) → Risk Management.**

## 2. Smart Contract Architecture

The prototype is written in Solidity (^0.8.20) and utilises standard OpenZeppelin libraries for security and compliance. It consists of three integrated contracts:

### A. RentToken (RNT)
*   **Standard:** ERC-20
*   **Function:** Represents fractionalised ownership of the rental income stream.
*   **Details:** A fixed supply of 1,000,000 RNT is minted upon deployment. Holding RNT entitles the user to a pro-rata share of the rental yields.

### B. TestUSD (TUSD)
*   **Standard:** ERC-20
*   **Function:** A mock stablecoin representing the actual fiat/stablecoin rental payments collected from tenants.
*   **Details:** Mints an initial supply for testing purposes to simulate the periodic cash flow generated by the underlying real estate.

### C. RentDistributor
*   **Function:** The core financial logic contract. It acts as the decentralised clearinghouse for cash flows.
*   **Mechanism:** The off-chain Asset Manager deposits `TestUSD` into this contract and calls `notifyRewardAmount()`. The contract algorithmically calculates the pro-rata share for each `RentToken` holder. Investors can independently call `getReward()` to claim their yield permissionlessly.

## 4. Transaction Flow (Proof of Concept)

To prove the economic logic, the following transaction flow can be executed on-chain:
1.  **Initialisation:** SPV deploys contracts and holds the total supply of RNT.
2.  **Market Distribution:** RNT tokens are transferred to various investor wallet addresses.
3.  **Yield Generation:** The Asset Manager transfers TUSD (representing collected rent) into the `RentDistributor` contract.
4.  **Notification:** The Asset Manager triggers `notifyRewardAmount()`, locking in the yield rate per token.
5.  **Claiming:** Investors call `getReward()` to withdraw their exact share of TUSD directly to their wallets.
